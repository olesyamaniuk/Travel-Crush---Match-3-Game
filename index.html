About the game

Travel Crush - Match 3 Game invites players on a thrilling world tour filled with exciting match-3 puzzles. Discover new countries, cultures, and landmarks as you match colorful tiles to unlock souvenirs and treasures. Whether you’re solving puzzles in Paris or relaxing on a beach in Bali, every level offers a new travel adventure. Enjoy hours of gameplay, beautiful graphics, and challenging puzzles as you explore the world from your screen!

How to Play

  • 1

    Match 3 or More: Swap tiles to match 3 or more of the same kind and clear them from the board.

  • 2

    Complete Travel Challenges: Each level has specific objectives, such as collecting souvenirs or unlocking new areas.

  • 3

    Use Power-Ups: Utilize special boosters and power-ups to clear tough levels and score higher.

  • 4

    Unlock New Locations: Progress through levels to unlock new destinations around the globe

  • 5

    Challenge Friends: Compare your progress with friends and compete for the highest score on social media.
